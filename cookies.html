Recipe #1 - PB Cup Chocolate Chip Blondies

  • Time: 1 Hour
  • Yield: 20 blondies
Ingredients Quantity
Brown Sugar 1 1/2 C
Butter (Melted) 3/4 C
Flour 1 1/2 C
Eggs 2
Baking Powder 1 tsp
Vanilla Extract 4 tsp
Peanut Butter Cups & Chocolate Chips 1/2 C each
Sea Salt 1/2 tsp

Step 1: Whisk together the flour, salt, and baking powder in a medium bowl. Set aside. In a seperate medium bowl, whisk together the brown sugar and melted butter. Add the eggs and vanilla.

Step 2: Using a spatula, fold the dry ingredients into the wet. Fold in the peanut butter chips and chocolate chips.

Step 3: Pour into a pan and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes. Let cool. Cut into bars and enjoy!

Recipe #2 - Buttercream

  • Time: 30 Minutes
  • Yield: 9-inch cake or 24 cupcakes
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1 C at room temp
Shortening 1 C
Vanilla Extract 1 Tbsp
Powdered Sugar 4 C
Milk 4 Tbsp

Step 1: Beat together butter and shortening until combined.

Step 2: Add in the vanilla extract and mix until just combined.

Step 3: Gradually add in powdered sugar.

Step 4: Add in milk one Tbsp at a time. Frost cakes, cupcakes, etc. and enjoy!

Recipe # 3 - Rice Krispie Treats

  • Time: 30 Minutes
  • Yield: 15 treats
Ingredients Quantity
Rice Krispies 6 C
Butter 6 Tbsp
Mini Marshmallows 6 C

Step 1:Melt butter and marshmallows in a medium pan over medium heat.

Step 2: Turn off heat and add in Rice Krispies.

Step 3: Cool until set. Cut into bars and enjoy!

Recipe #4 - Sugar Cookies

  • Time: 19 Minutes
  • Yield: 20 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Unsalted Butter 1/2 Cup at room temp
Sugar 1 Cup
Large Egg 1
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Flour 2 1/4 Cups
Salt 1/8 tsp
Baking Powder 1 1/2 tsp

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees and line a cookie sheet with a silicone baking mat or parchment paper.

Step 2: Cream butter and sugar together for 3-4 minutes, until light and fluffy.

Step 3: Add egg and vanilla extract and mix until combined.

Step 4: Combine dry ingredients in a separate bowl, then add to wet ingredients and mix until well combined. Dough will be very thick. You might need to use a spatula or your hands to help it all come together.

Step 5: On a lightly floured surface and using smaller amounts of dough at a time, roll out dough to about 1/8 to a 1/4 inch. If dough sticks to rolling pin, lightly sprinkle the top of the dough with flour.

Step 6: Use cookie cutters to cut out cookies in whatever shape you desire, then transfer to cookie sheet.

Step 7: Bake cookies 6-8 minutes. Remove from oven just before they start to brown on the edges.

Step 8: Allow cookies to cool for 4-5 minutes, then move to cooling rack to finish cooling.
