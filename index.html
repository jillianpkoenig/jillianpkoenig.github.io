Recipe #1 - PB Chocolate Chip Blondies

  • Time: 1 Hour
  • Serves: 20
Ingredients Quantity
Brown Sugar 1 1/2 C
Butter (Melted) 3/4 C
Flour 1 1/2 C
Eggs 2
Baking Powder 1 tsp
Vanilla Extract 4 tsp
Peanut butter & Chocolate chips 1/2 C each
Sea Salt 1/2 tsp

Step 1: Whisk together the flour, salt, and baking powder in a medium bowl. Set aside. In a seperate medium bowl, whisk together the brown sugar and melted butter. Add the eggs and vanilla.

Step 2: Using a spatula, fold the dry ingredients into the wet. Fold in the peanut butter chips and chocolate chips.

Step 3: Pour into a pan and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes. Let cool. Cut into bars and enjoy!

Recipe #2 - Buttercream

  • Time: 30 Minutes
  • Serves: 9-inch cake or 24 cupcakes
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1 C at room temp
Shortening 1 C
Vanilla Extract 1 Tbsp
Powdered Sugar 4 C
Milk 4 Tbsp

Step 1: Beat together butter and shortening until combined.

Step 2: Add in the vanilla extract and mix until just combined.

Step 3: Gradually add in powdered sugar.

Step 4: Add in milk one Tbsp at a time. Frost cakes, cupcakes, etc. and enjoy!

Recipe # 3 - Rice Krispie Treats

  • Time: 30 Hour
  • Serves: 15
Ingredients Quantity
Rice Krispies 6 C
Butter 6 Tbsp
Mini Marshmallows 6 C

Step 1:Melt butter and marshmallows in a medium pan over medium heat.

Step 2: Turn off heat and add in Rice Krispies.

Step 3: Cool until set. Cut into bars and enjoy!
